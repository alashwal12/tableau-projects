# HR Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes employee attrition and workforce demographics to support HR decision-making. 
It provides insights into attrition trends, employee age distribution, job satisfaction, and department-wise turnover.

---

## Objectives
- Monitor employee attrition and attrition rate
- Analyze attrition by department and education field
- Understand workforce age distribution
- Evaluate job satisfaction across job roles
- Compare attrition patterns by gender and age group

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes employee-level HR records with attributes such as:
- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Education Field
- Job Role
- Attrition Status
- Job Satisfaction

> Note: Raw data is not included due to privacy considerations.

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Active Employees
- Average Employee Age

---

## Tools Used
- Tableau Desktop
- Calculated Fields
- Interactive Filters & Parameters
